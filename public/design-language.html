§ 02 The Agency Desk

Run briefs
like headlines.

Write the brief once. Open it to the community and field qualified applicants, or walk it straight to a named creator and make the offer directly. Review by the book: fit scores, rate history, prior work, and terms side-by-side. Sign off, and production goes on the rails.
